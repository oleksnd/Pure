## ðŸ›  Features

### 1. Perception Matrix
An interactive 9x9 grid that provides instant psychological profiling for base colors and their specific shades. Choose a color to see its:
- **Psychological Profile:** The emotional core of the color.
- **Physical & Physiological Properties:** How it affects heart rate (HRV) and cognitive load.
- **Master's Advice:** When to use it and how it behaves in a composition.
- **Best Pairings:** Scientifically and aesthetically proven combinations.

### 2. Emotion Palettes
A library of "Color Strategies" designed to construct specific emotional environments:
- **Nostalgia:** Faded, low-saturation tones.
- **Serenity:** Parasympathetic-activating blues and greens.
- **Power:** High-dominance emeralds and golds.
- **Triumph:** Maximum arousal warm spectrums.

### 3. Design Formulas & Rules
Visualized guides for perfect balance:
- **60-30-10 Rule:** The golden ratio of harmony.
- **The Minimalist:** Absolute accent in a void.
- **The Duel:** Constructing tension through opposition.
- **The Monolith:** Total immersion in a single state.

### 4. Advanced Principles
- **Geometric Resonance:** How shapes (triangles/circles) amplify color impact (based on Kandinsky's theories).
- **Neural Opponency:** Using Hering's theory for physiological balance.
- **Circadian Validity:** Adjusting palettes for light spectrums and time of day.

### 5. Research & Data
A dedicated section for neurophysiological facts, covering:
- **Chromostereopsis:** The physics of "Push & Pull" in visual depth.
- **Memory Recall:** The 60% boost in retention through strategic color use.
